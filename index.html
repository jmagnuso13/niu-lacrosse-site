About the Program

NIU Huskies Men's Club Lacrosse is a student-led program competing against other Universities across the midwest. Play hard on Saturday afternoon, drink some gatorade, take some advil and find a way to play on Sunday morning.

  • Open to all NIU students — athletes of other sports welcome
  • Fall training and scrimmages & Spring League competition
  • Player-led culture with strong alumni support

A Proud History

Lacrosse is the oldest sports club at Northern Illinois University, first established in 1977. Graham and Ethan Shap, former D3 defensive standouts at Augustana, hope to rebuild on decades of Huskies tradition, and we're excited to write the next chapter together.

FAQ

Who can join?

All current NIU students. Prior HS experience is recommended, but we'll take any former HS athletes.

What season is this?

Lacrosse is primarily a Spring sport. Optional workouts and scrimmages can take place in the Fall.

What gear do I need?

Helmet, gloves, shoulder pads, arm pads, stick, and cleats. We’ll share detailed guidelines after you sign up.

Is there a cost?

$50 Club dues.
